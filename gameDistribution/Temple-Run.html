Temple Run

Temple Run is the iconic endless runner that started it all. Sprint through an ancient temple fleeing from a powerful guardian while collecting coins and power-ups. Master the art of quick reflexes, perfectly-timed jumps, and tactical turns. Every run pushes you to set a new personal record in this addictive classic that's challenging yet extremely rewarding.

How to Play

Swipe left or right to steer, swipe up to jump, and swipe down to slide. Navigate temple corridors, dodge obstacles, and collect coins. Use power-ups strategically and stay focused for high scores!

Tips for Success

Master the timing of your jumps and slides. Learn when to turn and when to go straight. Collect coins for upgrades and practice to build muscle memory for those critical moments!

Game Features

Endless gameplay, addictive mechanics, simple yet challenging controls, power-ups, coin collection, and high score tracking. A timeless classic that keeps you coming back!
