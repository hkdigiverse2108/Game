Money Movers

Money Movers is a co-op puzzle platformer where you control two characters with different abilities. Use teamwork, switch timing, and careful movement to avoid traps, collect money bags, and reach the exit together.

How to Play

Use separate controls for each character and move them in coordination through doors, lifts, switches, and hazards. Think ahead before each move so both players can survive and clear the puzzle.

What Happens Next

Later stages add tighter timing windows, more hazards, and trickier switch combinations. Plan routes for both characters and keep communication clear to avoid resetting the level.

Tips for Success

Start by identifying which character must open each path, then move in short safe steps. Prioritize survival first, then collect extra money bags once the route is under control.
