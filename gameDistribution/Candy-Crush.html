Candy Crush

Candy Crush is a colorful match-three puzzle game where every swap can trigger a chain reaction. Match candies of the same type, clear the board goals, and use smart placement to create the big combos that open up the level.

How to Play

Swap neighboring candies to make rows or columns of three or more. Build special candies, save powerful combinations for harder spots, and think a few moves ahead to finish each objective efficiently.

Tips for Success

Aim for matches near the bottom when you can, because those clears often create extra cascades. Save strong specials for tight boards and focus on the level goal instead of making random swaps.

Game Features

Bright candy visuals, satisfying match animations, combo potential, and a classic puzzle loop that stays fun whether you play a quick round or keep pushing through levels.
