Wood Block

Wood Block is Block Blast Part 2, so it uses the same clear and simple idea in a new wooden style. You place pieces on the board, try to finish full rows or columns, and keep enough open space for the next shapes. It is easy to learn, but the board can fill up fast if you do not plan ahead.

Simple Goal

Place each wooden piece carefully, clear lines whenever you can, and leave room for the next move. The more space you keep, the longer you can play.

How to Play

Drag a piece onto the board and drop it in a place where it fits. When a row or column is completely filled, it disappears and gives you more space. Try to think one move ahead so you do not block yourself with a hard shape.

Tips for Success

Save open space for large pieces, clear lines early, and avoid tiny gaps that are hard to use later. A clean board makes the game much easier.
