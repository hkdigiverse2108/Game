Breakout

Breakout is a classic arcade game where you control a paddle to bounce a ball and break through layers of bricks. Test your reflexes and strategy as you clear each level with precision and timing.

Break The Wall

Move your paddle to keep the ball in play and destroy all bricks to advance to the next level.

How to play

Use your mouse or arrow keys to move the paddle left and right. Keep the ball bouncing by positioning the paddle correctly. Break all bricks to complete each level and unlock new challenges.

Tips for success

Watch the ball's trajectory and anticipate its path. Use the walls and paddle angles strategically. Don't rush - sometimes a slower, more controlled approach leads to higher scores.
