2048 NumStack

2048 NumStack is a clean number-merging puzzle where every swipe shifts the whole board and creates new opportunities or new problems.

Match identical tiles to combine them into larger values, build chains carefully, and protect open cells so the grid never feels trapped too early.

The challenge is simple to learn but satisfying to master: one rushed move can block future merges, while patient planning can turn a crowded board into a huge score.

Think a few turns ahead, keep your highest values organized, and push toward the 2048 tile with steady, smart play.
