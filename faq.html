Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is Epic Games Hub?
Epic Games Hub is a comprehensive gaming platform where you can find and play a wide variety of games, all in one place. Our site features games from different genres, including action, puzzle, racing, and more.
2. Do I need to pay to play games on Epic Games Hub?
Epic Games Hub offers free games. All games on our platform are available to play at no cost.
3. How can I contact customer support?
If you need assistance or have any questions, you can contact our support team through our Contact Page. We aim to respond as quickly as possible.
4. Are my personal details safe with Epic Games Hub?
Yes, we take your privacy seriously. We use reasonable security measures to protect your information. For more details, please review our Privacy Policy.
5. How do I report a problem with a game?
If you encounter any issues with a game, please visit our Contact Page and provide details about the problem. Our team will review and resolve it as soon as possible.
6. Can I play games on Epic Games Hub using a mobile device?
Yes, Epic Games Hub is optimized for both desktop and mobile devices. You can play games on your smartphone or tablet directly through your browser.
7. How often are new games added to Epic Games Hub?
We regularly update our library with new and exciting games. Check the homepage or “New Games” section to stay updated.
8. Can I suggest a game for Epic Games Hub?
Yes, we welcome suggestions from users. You can submit your ideas through our Contact Page.
9. What should I do if I encounter technical issues?
If you face technical issues, try clearing your browser cache and updating your browser. If the problem continues, contact our support team for help.
