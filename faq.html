Frequently Asked Questions

Are all games completely free?

Yes! Every single game on PlayBox is 100% free to play. We sustain the platform through lightweight, non-intrusive advertising.

Do I need to download anything?

No downloads or installations are required. Everything runs instantly inside your web browser using modern HTML5 technology.

My game progress was lost, what happened?

Game progress is currently saved locally in your browser's cache. If you cleared your browser history/cookies, or are playing in Incognito/Private mode, your saves may be wiped. We are working on cloud saving for registered users soon!

Can I play these games on my phone?

Absolutely. The PlayBox website and almost all of our games are strictly optimized for mobile web browsers, ensuring you get a seamless experience on both iOS and Android.
