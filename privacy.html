Privacy Policy

At PlayBox, we take your privacy seriously. This document outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when you interact with our platform.

1. Data Collection

We only collect essential data necessary to provide a seamless gaming experience, including basic analytics (such as page views, game playtime, and interaction events) to help us improve our offerings. We do not require account registration for basic gameplay.

2. Cookies

We use localized browser storage and non-intrusive cookies to remember your game progress, save your visual preferences (like dark mode), and manage recent searches. You have full control to clear these via your browser settings at any time.

3. Third-Party Games

Our platform hosts games created by third-party developers. While we strictly moderate content, some games may execute their own temporary localized logic. We do not share any personal identifying information with these third-party entities.
